# AI-Fluent Leader — Overview

This wiki is a personal knowledge base for becoming an **AI-fluent leader** and pursuing AI leadership roles (CAIO, Head of AI, AI strategy lead). It is populated automatically by three remote Claude Code routines that send daily/weekly briefings via email and also commit Karpathy-style wiki pages to this repo.

## Two lanes the wiki tracks

- **Strategy** — high-level enterprise AI trends, executive frameworks, market signals. Primary curator: [[nate-b-jones]].
- **Implementation** — hands-on automation, Claude Code workflows, practical builds. Primary curator: [[nate-herk]].

A weekly digest also pulls cross-platform stories (HN, Reddit, Substack, McKinsey/Deloitte/Gartner/WEF reports, AI leadership job postings) to build a broader picture of the field.

## How to read this wiki

- **Start in [[index]]** to see every page grouped by type.
- Each email summary is a **source page** under `wiki/sources/`.
- People and orgs live under `wiki/entities/`.
- Ideas and frameworks live under `wiki/concepts/`.
- Filed synthesis lives under `wiki/analyses/`.
