# Anthropic

**Anthropic** — AI safety and research company, founded by Dario Amodei and Daniela Amodei. Structured as a **Public Benefit Corporation (PBC)**, meaning it is legally chartered to balance commercial success with social good.

## Governance Structure

Anthropic operates a distinctive governance layer: the **Long-Term Benefit Trust (LTBT)**, an independent body that holds Anthropic accountable to its mission. Trustees:
- Hold **no equity** in Anthropic
- Receive **no share of profits**
- Are chosen by **existing trustees**, not by management or investors
- Represent a range of expertise: economics, policy, academia, public service

## LTBT Appointments (2026)

- **Ben Bernanke** appointed July 9, 2026 — Nobel Prize in Economics 2022, former Federal Reserve Chair (2006–2014), Distinguished Fellow at Brookings Institution. Bernanke's expertise in systemic economic risk and crisis management maps onto Anthropic's view that AI carries macroeconomic consequences at scale.

## Relevance to AI-Fluent Leadership

Anthropic's LTBT is a reference architecture for structurally independent AI governance — a model enterprise leaders can adapt: expert trustees with no financial stake, mandate to balance commercial and societal outcomes, multi-disciplinary composition (not just technologists).
