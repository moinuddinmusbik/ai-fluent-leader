# Samsung

South Korean electronics and technology conglomerate. Samsung Electronics is one of the largest single-employer enterprise AI deployments tracked in this wiki, spanning multiple AI vendors.

## Key Facts

- Imposed a company-wide ChatGPT ban in 2023 after engineers leaked sensitive source code through the tool
- Samsung SDS is Samsung's IT-services affiliate, now also an OpenAI reseller
- Samsung SDS also uses Claude (Anthropic) through affiliate deployments

## ChatGPT Enterprise and Codex Rollout (June 21, 2026)

- OpenAI is rolling out ChatGPT Enterprise and Codex to every Samsung Electronics employee in South Korea, plus all Device eXperience (DX) division staff worldwide.
- The deal reverses Samsung's 2023 ChatGPT ban, following Codex weekly active users in Korea growing nearly 800% since February 2026 through organic/shadow usage.
- Samsung SDS becomes the first Korean entity authorized to resell and manage ChatGPT Enterprise for other businesses.
- Rollout spans R&D, manufacturing, marketing, and management support, with global workforce training targeted for completion by year-end 2026.

## Relevance to AI-Fluent Leadership

Samsung is a live case study in the "shadow AI usage curve" problem: organic Codex adoption hit 800% growth before governance caught up, and the formal rollout is explicitly catching up to behavior that was already happening. Leaders auditing their own AI footprint should treat unsanctioned usage growth as a leading indicator that a formal rollout decision is already overdue.
