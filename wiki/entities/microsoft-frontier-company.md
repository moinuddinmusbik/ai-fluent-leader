# Microsoft Frontier Company

**Type:** Corporate organizational unit
**Parent:** Microsoft
**Announced:** July 2, 2026
**Investment:** $2.5 billion
**Headcount:** ~6,000

## What It Is
A dedicated enterprise AI deployment unit that embeds specialists directly inside large customer organizations for 6–12 month engagements. Personnel include ~2,000 solution architects, 1,800 deployment engineers, 1,200 trainers, and 1,000 strategists; all undergo a six-week AI induction.

## Why It Exists
MIT Project NANDA: 95% of enterprise generative AI pilots deliver zero measurable P&L impact. Microsoft recognized the hardest part of enterprise AI is no longer model access but organizational change management and last-mile deployment.

## Strategic Significance
Inverts Microsoft's prior model — the product is now successful deployment, not license access. Signals that model access has been commoditized; organizational change management is the new competitive moat.
