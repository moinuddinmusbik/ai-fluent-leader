# Microsoft

Enterprise technology platform company; primary cloud and AI platform provider for large organizations. Owns Azure (cloud), M365 (productivity), GitHub (developer tools), and Copilot (AI assistant layer).

## Key Facts

- CEO: Satya Nadella
- AI strategy lead: Mustafa Suleyman (Microsoft AI)
- Core AI platform: Azure OpenAI Service + proprietary MAI model family (as of June 2026)
- Revenue signal: enterprise AI is on track to reach parity with consumer AI revenue by end of 2026

## Build 2026 Announcements (June 2–3, 2026)

The defining enterprise AI event of the week of June 1–7, 2026:
- **Autopilots**: always-on autonomous agents with their own Entra ID (see [[enterprise-ai-autopilots]])
- **Microsoft IQ**: unified intelligence layer — Work IQ, Foundry IQ, Fabric IQ Ontology, Web IQ
- **MAI model family**: MAI-Thinking-1 (reasoning), MAI-Code-1 (coding), MAI-Transcribe-1.5 (43 languages), MAI-Voice-2
- **Project Solara**: agent OS treating agents as first-class enterprise actors
- **Maia 200**: second-gen AI accelerators operational in Iowa, Arizona; Italy, Australia, South Korea upcoming

## Leadership Implications

Microsoft's Build 2026 signals that enterprise AI is consolidating onto a unified stack — organizations fragmented across point solutions face growing governance and cost complexity.
