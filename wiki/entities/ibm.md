# IBM

**International Business Machines Corporation** — multinational technology company and one of the primary producers of enterprise AI research through its **IBM Institute for Business Value (IBV)**.

## Relevance to AI-Fluent Leadership

IBM is a key data source for enterprise AI adoption trends. Its IBV studies survey thousands of C-suite executives and produce the most cited quantitative benchmarks for AI governance maturity, CAIO adoption rates, and enterprise readiness gaps.

## Key Facts (2026)

- Published the **2026 Tech Leader Study** (July 8, 2026) surveying 2,000 C-level technology executives globally
- Key finding: **2/3 of CIOs/CTOs are accountable for AI systems they don't fully control**
- 76% of organisations globally have now appointed a CAIO (up from 26% one year prior)
- 77% of organisations say AI adoption is outpacing governance
- Only 11% of tech leaders feel fully ready for agentic AI deployment at the expected 2027 scale
- Published **2026 CEO Study: Rewiring the C-Suite** — focusing on C-suite alignment for AI transformation
