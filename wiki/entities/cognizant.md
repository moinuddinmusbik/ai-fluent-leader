# Cognizant

**Cognizant Technology Solutions** (Nasdaq: CTSH) — global professional services and IT company headquartered in Teaneck, NJ. One of the world's largest enterprise AI deployment firms.

## Relevance to AI-Fluent Leadership

Cognizant represents a case study in the "AI Builder" strategy: use AI internally at scale first, then export the proven model to clients. Its 200,000-associate Gemini Enterprise rollout (July 2026) is among the largest single enterprise AI deployments announced this year.

## Key Facts (2026)

- **AI Builder strategy**: competitive differentiation via speed-to-production, not tool selection
- **Frontier Certified Engineer** delivery model: AI specialists who audit workflows, evaluate agents, and deploy directly into production — not just build prototypes
- **Partnership**: expanded Google Cloud collaboration (July 7, 2026) for Gemini Enterprise + Google Workspace across all 200,000 associates
- **Antigravity 2.0**: internal tool combining Gemini capabilities for code explanation, automated test generation, and legacy application modernisation
- **Internal benchmark**: up to 30% improvement in software development velocity
- CEO: Ravi Kumar S
