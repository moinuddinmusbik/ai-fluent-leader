# Ben Bernanke

**Dr. Ben S. Bernanke** — Nobel Prize in Economic Sciences (2022), former Chair of the Federal Reserve (2006–2014), Distinguished Fellow at the Brookings Institution.

## AI Leadership Relevance

Appointed July 9, 2026 to **Anthropic's Long-Term Benefit Trust (LTBT)**. His selection signals a deliberate institutional strategy: recruiting systemic risk economists — not only AI researchers — to govern AI's development.

Bernanke's expertise: how economies absorb and react to structural disruptions (Great Depression research; steering the global economy through the 2008 financial crisis). Directly applicable to AI's macroeconomic impact at scale.

> "The potential of artificial intelligence is enormous, and so is the range of outcomes. How that potential plays out will depend, in part, on the institutions we build around it." — Ben Bernanke, July 2026
