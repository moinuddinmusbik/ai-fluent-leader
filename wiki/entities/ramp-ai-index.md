# Ramp AI Index

Monthly enterprise AI adoption tracker published by Ramp (corporate card and spend management platform). Unique because it is derived from **actual credit-card and billing data** across tens of thousands of US companies — not survey-based, not self-reported.

## Why It Matters for AI-Fluent Leaders

The Ramp AI Index is one of the few public data sources that reflects real enterprise AI spend rather than marketing claims, pilot announcements, or survey preferences. It tracks which AI products businesses actually pay for month-over-month, making it a leading indicator of where enterprise AI budgets are flowing.

## Key Findings (June 2026)

- Anthropic at **41% of US businesses with paid AI subscriptions** — first time Anthropic has topped the Ramp AI Index, overtaking OpenAI.
- OpenAI adoption declining month-over-month despite IPO momentum and Oracle distribution announcement.
- Adoption curve for Anthropic: <1% (June 2023) → 34.4% (April 2026) → 41% (June 2026) — an unprecedented enterprise software adoption velocity.
- Shift driven by Claude's performance on complex, long-context, and multi-step agentic tasks.
