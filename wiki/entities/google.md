# Google

AI platform and cloud company; one of the dominant enterprise AI vendors in 2026.

## Key Facts

- At Google I/O 2026 (May 20), launched Gemini 3.5 Flash: strongest agentic and coding model yet at <50% competitor cost
- Launched Gemini Spark: a 24/7 personal AI agent explicitly positioned as an “AI employee” for enterprise use
- Launched Antigravity 2.0: enterprise platform for orchestrating agent workforces, replacing Gemini CLI
- Managed Agents in Gemini API: single API call provisions a full Linux environment for autonomous agent tasks
- Deep Workspace integration: for Google Workspace organizations, agentic AI is available natively as of May 20, 2026

## Relevance to AI-Fluent Leadership

Google I/O 2026 represents the moment enterprise AI moved from “tools” to “AI employees” framing. The economics of Gemini 3.5 Flash make agentic deployment viable at scale for any Workspace organization. Leaders need to engage with this not as a future roadmap item but as a current deployment decision.
