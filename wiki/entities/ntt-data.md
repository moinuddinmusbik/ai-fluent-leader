# NTT DATA

Japanese multinational IT and systems integration firm; one of the world's largest SI firms by revenue (~$33B). Parent: NTT Group.

## Relevance to AI-Fluent Leader

NTT DATA is a primary case study in how large-scale systems integrators are positioning as the industrialisation layer between hyperscaler AI platforms and enterprise deployment at scale.

## Key AI Leadership Actions (2026)

- **June 8, 2026**: Announced expanded Google Cloud partnership to deploy up to 500 AI agents across enterprise verticals (banking, insurance, manufacturing, retail) using Gemini Enterprise.
- Establishing a **Gemini Enterprise Practice** with 5,000 certified AI engineers — a talent-first strategy that separates NTT DATA from pure-technology competitors.
- Introducing a **global factory model** for agent development: reusable assets, industrialised deployment, "forward-deployed engineers" embedded with clients.
- Prioritising sovereign and compliant AI deployments for regulated industries — data residency, regulatory, and compliance guarantees built-in.
- June 12, 2026: Announced a new executive leadership team (internal restructuring noted alongside AI practice announcements).
