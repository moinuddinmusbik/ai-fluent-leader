# Hang Ten Systems

Enterprise AI transformation firm founded in June 2026 by [[vishal-sikka]] (former CEO of Infosys).

## Key Facts
- **Founded:** June 2026
- **Seed funding:** $32 million
- **Founder:** Vishal Sikka — former CEO of Infosys, former CTO of SAP
- **Mission:** Help enterprises build and operate AI-driven software at production scale
- **Market thesis:** Enterprise AI adoption has created a massive gap between aspiration and execution; managed AI transformation services are the next large market

## Relevance to AI-Fluent Leadership
Hang Ten Systems represents the market consensus that the 2026 enterprise AI challenge is not tool access but implementation expertise. The $32M seed round validates that:
- Enterprises lack internal capability to operationalize AI at scale
- Managed transformation services are preferred over point-solution sprawl
- The CIO community is consolidating toward fewer, deeper vendor relationships
