# HDFC Bank

India's **largest private sector bank**, headquartered in Mumbai. FY2025-26 marked as its "transformational phase" — the shift from digitising banking to building an AI-powered enterprise.

## Neev Platform

**Neev** — HDFC Bank's proprietary full-stack enterprise GenAI platform (launched/announced July 2026):
- Provides: model access, governance, orchestration, and deployment as shared infrastructure
- Covers: customer service, lending, wealth management, internal operations
- Design philosophy: one common foundation for all departments (vs. departmental tool sprawl)
- Integrations: Mobile Banking, NetBanking, WhatsApp Banking
- Capabilities: personalised responses, fraud detection, multilingual support, data-driven lending insights
- Companion product: **RMPro** — unified CRM for relationship managers with AI-layer insights

## Relevance to AI-Fluent Leadership

HDFC Bank's Neev is a case study in the "platform over proliferation" approach to enterprise AI: governance, scalability, and regulatory compliance are built into the infrastructure layer rather than bolted onto individual deployments.
