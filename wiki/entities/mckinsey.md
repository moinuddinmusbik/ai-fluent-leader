# McKinsey & Company

Global management consulting firm; leading publisher of enterprise AI research including the State of AI and State of Organizations annual reports.

## Key Facts
- **Headquarters:** New York, USA
- **Founded:** 1926
- **Practice areas:** Strategy, operations, organization, technology, digital transformation
- **AI research:** Annual State of AI report (quantitative survey, widely cited); State of Organizations report (leadership and org design)

## AI Leadership Coverage
McKinsey is a primary source for:
- Enterprise AI adoption benchmarks (% of orgs using AI, by function)
- AI maturity and ROI data
- Organizational design for AI (operating model redesign, role evolution)
- Agentic AI deployment patterns and scaling rates

## Notable Findings (June 2026)
McKinsey's State of Organizations 2026 found:
- 86% of leaders unprepared for agentic AI
- Trailblazers 2× more likely to scale
- Only 12% of orgs have redesigned operating models at scale
- AI agents direct 50%+ of AI investment at leading organizations
