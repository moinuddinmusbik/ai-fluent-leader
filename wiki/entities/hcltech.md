# HCLTech

Global technology company (NSE/BSE: HCLTECH), publisher of enterprise AI market research.

## Key Facts

- Published AI Impact Imperatives 2026 (May 20, 2026): survey of 467 senior leaders across G2K organizations in 10 countries
- Found 43% of enterprise AI initiatives at meaningful risk of failure due to leadership and change management gaps
- Identified change management as the most consistently underinvested area in enterprise AI programs
- Report concludes the next phase of AI will test leadership readiness and people readiness at scale, not technology readiness

## Relevance to AI-Fluent Leadership

HCLTech’s research provides current large-scale data on why enterprise AI programs fail. The 43% failure risk figure and the change-management finding are critical benchmarks for any leader assessing their own program’s risk profile.
