# World Economic Forum (WEF)

Global intergovernmental and multi-stakeholder organization convening governments, businesses, and civil society. Hosts the annual Davos summit (January) and the Annual Meeting of the New Champions (Summer Davos, June/July in China).

## Key Facts
- **Headquarters:** Cologny/Geneva, Switzerland
- **Founded:** 1971 by Klaus Schwab
- **Key events:** Annual Meeting in Davos (Jan); Annual Meeting of the New Champions (Summer Davos, June)
- **AI relevance:** Major publisher of AI workforce, governance, and leadership research; convener of AI-focused sessions at both annual summits

## AI Leadership Coverage
WEF is a primary source for:
- Global AI workforce impact data (job displacement, reskilling timelines)
- Leadership and succession planning in the AI era
- Cross-sector AI governance frameworks
- AI talent and wage premium data

## Notable Findings (June 2026)
At Summer Davos 2026, WEF surfaced Harvard research showing entry-level job postings dropped 80%/quarter at AI-adopting organizations — raising the [[ai-leadership-pipeline-risk]] concept as a board-level strategic concern. See: [[2026-06-28-weekly-ai-leadership-stories]].
