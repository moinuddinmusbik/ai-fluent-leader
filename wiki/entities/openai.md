# OpenAI

AI research and deployment company; creator of the GPT model family, ChatGPT, and the Codex coding agent. Anthropic's primary frontier-lab rival for enterprise AI deployment share.

## Key Facts

- Creator of ChatGPT Enterprise and the Codex agentic coding tool
- Pursuing large-scale enterprise rollouts as a core go-to-market strategy alongside API and consumer products
- Samsung SDS is now the first Korean entity authorized to resell and manage ChatGPT Enterprise for other businesses

## Samsung Electronics Deployment (June 21, 2026)

- Rolling out ChatGPT Enterprise and Codex to every Samsung Electronics employee in South Korea, plus all Device eXperience (DX) division staff worldwide — described by OpenAI as "one of OpenAI's largest enterprise launches ever."
- The deal reverses a 2023 company-wide ChatGPT ban Samsung imposed after engineers leaked sensitive source code through the tool — signaling enterprise security postures have matured enough to reopen access at scale.
- Codex weekly active users in Korea grew nearly 800% since February 2026 — the formal rollout is catching up to usage that was already accelerating organically.
- Samsung SDS becomes the first Korean entity authorized to resell and manage ChatGPT Enterprise for other businesses, turning Samsung into both customer and distribution channel for OpenAI in Korea.
- Samsung plans to apply the tools across R&D, manufacturing, marketing, and management support, with full global workforce training targeted for completion by year-end 2026.

## Relevance to AI-Fluent Leadership

The Samsung deal is a template for "ban-to-scale" reversal: organizations that pulled back from a tool after a security incident can revisit that decision once enterprise-grade controls genuinely exist, rather than treating the ban as permanent policy. The reseller structure (Samsung SDS) also previews a pattern AI-fluent leaders should watch for — large enterprise AI deployments becoming internal capability that the customer turns into a distribution channel of its own.
