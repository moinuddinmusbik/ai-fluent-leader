# AI Productivity Multiplier

The measurable ratio of output-per-person-per-period for AI-native teams vs. their pre-AI baseline. The reference benchmark from Anthropic's June 2026 "When AI Builds Itself" report: **8×** (same engineering headcount, 8× the code shipped per quarter).

## The 8× Benchmark

As of May 2026, Anthropic engineers ship 8× more code per quarter than the 2021–2025 baseline with the same headcount. This is the first public, quantified productivity multiplier from a frontier AI lab operating at scale.

Key data points:
- >80% of merged code authored by Claude (vs. low single digits pre-2025)
- 8× output per engineer per quarter
- 76% Claude success rate on open-ended tasks (up 50 percentage points in 6 months)

## The Compounding Risk

Teams compounding at 8× vs. 1.2× become structurally incomparable within 2–3 years. This is not a marginal performance gap — it is a capability cliff. The AI-fluent leader's job is to close the gap before it becomes permanent.

## How to Apply This Framework

1. **Establish your baseline now**: output per person per quarter (code shipped, documents produced, decisions made, tasks completed)
2. **Apply the 80% threshold question**: which of your professional functions could have 80% of their output volume generated by AI within 18 months?
3. **Brief the board as a competitive risk**: frame productivity compounding as a structural threat, not a technology upgrade
