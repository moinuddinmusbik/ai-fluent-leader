# Enterprise AI Autopilots

Microsoft's term for always-on autonomous AI agents that operate with their own enterprise identity (Entra ID), introduced at Microsoft Build 2026 (June 2–3, 2026).

## Definition

Unlike Copilot-style assistants that respond to prompts, Autopilots:
- Run continuously in the background without requiring a human trigger
- Hold their own Entra ID — every action is attributable to a known actor in the enterprise directory
- Manage their own memory, permissions, and task queues via Project Solara (agent OS)
- Operate within the Microsoft IQ unified intelligence layer: Work IQ, Foundry IQ, Fabric IQ Ontology, Web IQ

## Why This Matters for Leaders

The identity requirement is the architectural shift. Giving an agent its own Entra ID means:
- Audit trails exist for every agent action (compliance-critical)
- Agents can be provisioned, deprovisioned, and governed like employees
- The enterprise security perimeter extends to AI agents, not just humans
