# Building in Public

The practice of sharing work-in-progress AI builds, automations, and learnings publicly (LinkedIn, GitHub, Twitter/X) while still developing them — creating a visible, auditable track record that functions as a portfolio for job transitions and credibility-building.

## Why It Matters

A traditional resume lists titles and years of experience. "Building in public" creates a living body of real, demonstrable work. In the AI era, when the pivotal interview question is "What have you built?", a public build trail answers it conclusively while credentials and certifications cannot.

## The Mechanism

Each public build simultaneously serves as:
1. **A practice rep** — executing and iterating on real automations sharpens skills faster than tutorials.
2. **A proof point** — a potential employer, client, or partner can audit the actual work, not just claims.
3. **A compounding signal** — each new build extends the trail and increases overall credibility.
