# AI Agency Playbook

Framework for building an AI services business with lasting enterprise value, as articulated by Nate Herk and Devin Kearns (Custom AI Studio).

## Core Principles

1. **Vertical depth beats horizontal breadth** — own one process category end-to-end rather than selling generic AI capability.
2. **Mid-market is the 2026–2027 sweet spot** — $50M–$500M revenue; real budget, accessible CXOs, not yet hyperscaler-locked.
3. **Price on displacement** — calculate the process cost today (FTEs + rework); target 30–50% reduction as your value proposition.
4. **Discovery-first packaging** — fixed-fee discovery ($15–25k) that funds a demonstrable prototype; retainer on outcome metrics.
5. **Claude Code as the build layer** — compresses delivery timelines; small teams can compete with large SIs.
