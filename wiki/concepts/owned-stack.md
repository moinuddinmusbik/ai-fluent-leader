# The Owned Stack

Nate B. Jones's three-layer model for personal AI infrastructure that you build and control, rather than renting from a platform.

Introduced: [[2026-07-14-nate-b-jones-daily]]  
Related: [[one-loop]] · [[agent-intent]] · [[nate-b-jones]]

## The Three Layers

- **Open Brain** — The memory layer: what the agent retains about you, your context, your preferences, and your recurring situations across all sessions and models
- **Open Skills** — The method layer: the defined procedures and behavioral rules the agent executes on your behalf (your method, not a platform default)
- **Open Engine** — The work layer: the actual task execution system that moves work in the external world

## The Core Frame

"Rented intelligence on top, owned context underneath."

The intelligence (the model) can come from OpenAI, Anthropic, or any provider. What you own is the context, memory, and intent layer underneath. This prevents any single platform from controlling what your AI knows about you and how it reads your instructions.

## Why Build It Now

- The capability gap closed between February and July 2026
- Coding agents (Claude Code, Codex) can walk the setup by conversation — ~80% buildable without CLI expertise
- Platforms (OpenClaw, Hermes, Apple, OpenAI, Anthropic) will otherwise make this decision for you
