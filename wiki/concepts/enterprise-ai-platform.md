# Enterprise AI Platform

A **shared AI infrastructure layer** that provides model access, governance, orchestration, and deployment capabilities as common services across an entire organisation — as opposed to siloed, departmental AI tools.

## Why Platform Beats Proliferation

| Approach | Outcome |
|---|---|
| Departmental tool sprawl | Shadow AI, inconsistent governance, duplication of cost, security gaps |
| Shared enterprise platform | Unified governance, compound capability, regulatory compliance by design, scalable deployment |

## 2026 Examples

### HDFC Bank — Neev
Full-stack GenAI platform: model access + governance + orchestration + deployment. Covers customer service, lending, wealth management, internal ops. One foundation for all departments; each team builds AI-powered applications within consistent security and regulatory standards.

### Cognizant — Gemini Enterprise + Antigravity 2.0
Internal deployment of Google Gemini Enterprise to 200,000+ associates, combined with Antigravity 2.0 for software engineering automation. Proven internally first, then exported to clients via Frontier Certified Engineers.

## Key Architecture Layers

1. **Model access** — standardised interface to LLMs/AI models
2. **Governance** — data rules, access controls, compliance, audit trails
3. **Orchestration** — workflow automation, agent coordination, multi-step pipelines
4. **Deployment** — production-grade infrastructure for reliable, monitored AI output
