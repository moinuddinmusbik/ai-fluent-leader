# Enterprise Context Platform

An emerging AI platform category defined by the ability to ingest, maintain, and reason over an organization's full institutional knowledge at scale — the "trillion-token organizational context" layer.

## Why it matters

The company that first makes enterprise-scale organizational context genuinely usable doesn't just sell AI features; it becomes the new enterprise data platform with lock-in that structurally exceeds prior generations of enterprise software (SAP, Salesforce, Oracle).

## Key properties

- **Intelligence × Context multiplicativity:** Both reasoning quality and context depth must be high — weak reasoning with long context actively harms outputs
- **RAG inadequacy:** Standard retrieval augmentation breaks at enterprise scale in ways that curated benchmarks do not measure
- **Living memory:** Must handle continuously evolving organizational knowledge, not static snapshots
- **Compounding lock-in:** Synthesized organizational understanding compounds over time, making switching costs grow nonlinearly

## Current competitors

- **OpenAI** — infrastructure-first play; ChatGPT 5.4 signals trillion-token context roadmap; four-part compound bet underpins $840B valuation
- **Anthropic** — organic context accumulation through Claude Code usage patterns; synthesized understanding approach
