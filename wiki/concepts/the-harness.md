# The Harness

A framework introduced by [[nate-b-jones]] to name the company layer that sits between AI model intelligence and real organizational value.

## Definition

> "The model supplies intelligence. The harness supplies the company: the context, documents, permissions, review standards, memory, budgets, decision rights, and accountability that make intelligence useful in a real organization."
> — Nate B. Jones, [[2026-06-14-nate-b-jones-daily]]

The harness is everything around the model that makes intelligence usable and trustworthy in your specific organization. The labs can sell you models, tools, and even human engineers to install them. They cannot sell you your own operating context or the judgment that decides when an output is good enough to ship.

## Components

- **Context:** Company-specific documents, institutional history, domain knowledge
- **Permissions:** Who can trigger what actions, at what threshold of confidence
- **Review standards:** What "good enough to ship" means in your organization
- **Memory:** Institutional knowledge accumulated across AI interactions
- **Budgets:** Cost controls and resource allocation for AI operations
- **Decision rights:** Which outputs require human sign-off and at what level
- **Accountability:** Who is responsible when AI outputs are wrong

## Why It Matters

As AI model costs collapse (97% drops in one month are documented), value does not evaporate — it shifts to whoever owns the harness. The labs are racing to become harness owners alongside model providers. Your choice is whether the harness your organization runs on is one you own or one you rent.

## Related Concepts

- **Two-World Thesis** (same episode): Public markets price intelligence as scarce; operating reality shows costs collapsing. Both are true simultaneously.
- **Forward deployed engineering:** The lab practice of embedding human engineers customer by customer to install AI workflows — an implicit admission that building a harness requires human judgment.
- **Recursive self-improvement:** At company scale, this is the iteration-speed flywheel on your harness: tight feedback loops between AI output and your judgment standards.
