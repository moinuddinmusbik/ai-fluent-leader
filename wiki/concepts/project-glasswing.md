# Project Glasswing

Anthropic's restricted access program for Claude Mythos-5 — the unconstrained version of the Fable-5 / Mythos model family.

- Grants access to Claude Mythos-5: same model as Fable-5 but with cyber safeguards lifted
- Participants: a small group of cyber defenders and US government partners only
- Commercial users do not have access to Mythos-5 under any plan
- Creates a structural capability gap between commercial (Fable-5) and defense/government (Mythos-5) users
- Named in context of the June 9, 2026 dual-model launch by Anthropic
