## What It Means For You
1. **Score labs on the month, not the week.** Track pre-training cadence and talent retention as your leading indicators — not press cycles.
2. **Investigate marquee hires instead of reacting to them.** Ask what specific capability gap a "Shazeer-style" move is meant to close before updating your vendor risk read.
3. **Widen your competitive scan past the two incumbents.** This week's most consequential signal may be an applied breakthrough (Midjourney/medical imaging) outside the frontier-model race — don't let the OpenAI-vs-Anthropic frame crowd it out.
4. **Stress-test headline-driven AI strategy decisions.** When a model-race headline lands, ask whether it changes your operating reality this quarter — or just the news cycle.

## Mistake

Nate's framing treats that as a category error: the real signal is multi-week, built from pre-training cadence and where talent is actually flowing, not from who dominated this week's press cycle.
