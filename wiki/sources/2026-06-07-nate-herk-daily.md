## What Happened

A "Mythos" model identifier appeared briefly on Anthropic's live API on 2026-06-07, was screenshotted widely, then vanished. Nate uses this as the hook to explain what Mythos actually is, why the hype is structurally wrong, and what builders should actually be watching.

**What Mythos is:** The model tier above [[opus-4-8]]. Anthropic's internal draft called it "a step change — the most powerful model they've ever built." Specialized in cybersecurity: finding and patching security holes in old, widely-used code. The dual-use risk (find holes to patch them = find holes to exploit them) is why it's locked down.

**Project Glasswing:** Anthropic's invite-only rollout. Started ~50 vetted cybersecurity partners (April 2026) → expanded to 150 organizations across 15+ countries by June 2026. The expansion rate is the key signal.

**Pricing (Glasswing):** $25/M input tokens, $125/M output — 5x Opus 4.8 today.

---

## Nate's Take

No public Mythos is coming — at least not as a product called Mythos. Anthropic told us in April: "We do not plan to make Mythos preview generally available." Nothing has walked that back.

His **base case**: Mythos capabilities fold silently into a future Opus version. Most users never realize it's happening. He gives this the most probability.

His **bull case**: Competitive pressure (GPT-5.6 timing + IPO momentum) forces a limited, gated, expensive Mythos later in 2026 — but toned down, not the full Glasswing power level.

His **bear case**: Mythos stays locked to vetted Glasswing partners essentially forever, as a permanent defensive security tool.

---

## His Reasoning (with receipts)

1. **Explicit statement:** Anthropic April 2026 — "We do not plan to make Mythos preview generally available."
2. **Weapon risk:** Dual-use cybersecurity capability justifies permanent gatekeeping.
3. **IPO hype motive:** $965B confidential filing (June 1) + "AI improving itself" report (June 4) + Mythos leak (June 7) = PR sequencing with a motive. "Hype has a motive right now."
4. **Call-for-pause contradiction:** Report calls for global AI treaty to slow Frontier AI while Anthropic expands Glasswing weekly. Nate's read: not pure hypocrisy ("we'll slow down if everyone slows down together"), but the timing is suspicious.
5. **Price:** $125/M output = niche enterprise, not mass-market.
6. **Nate's own clarification (pinned comment):** "Mythos-class models in the coming weeks" from Opus 4.8 announcement = safeguarded version only, "once stronger cyber safeguards are in place."

## What It Means For Builders

- **Toolchain ceiling = Opus 4.8.** Build against that. Mythos-class capability arrives inside a future Opus silently.
- **5x pricing signal** = plan for model-agnosticism now as a cost-risk hedge.
- **GPT-5.6 is the real forcing function.** >80% betting odds by end of June 2026. Anthropic's response window (same day or within days) is when capability surprises happen.
- **Safety gating shapes access tiers** even post-"launch."

## Steal This For Your Org

1. **5x-cost stress test** — which Opus workflows break if pricing follows Glasswing signal?

## Mistake to Avoid
