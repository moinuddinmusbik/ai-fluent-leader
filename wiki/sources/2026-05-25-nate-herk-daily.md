# The Playbook for a $100M AI Agency

**Video:** [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8ktcSaSTvxk) · Published 2026-05-25 IST · Nate Herk ft. Devin Kearns (Custom AI Studio)

## Summary

Nate and Devin Kearns unpack what separates AI agencies with lasting enterprise value from lifestyle businesses that won't survive 2027. The mid-market ($50M–$500M revenue) is the prime opportunity: real pain, real budget, not yet locked into hyperscaler AI contracts. The durable AI agency offer replaces one repeatable expensive process end-to-end — priced on outcomes (saved FTE hours, error-rate reduction), never time-and-materials.

## Key Takeaways

- **2027 cliff:** Generic GPT wrappers have no moat; consolidation to vertical-workflow owners is underway.
- **Mid-market sweet spot:** $50M–$500M revenue — real budget, accessible CXOs, no hyperscaler lock-in yet.
- **$100M offer formula:** One repeatable expensive process → supervised AI loop → charge on displacement value.
- **Claude Code as delivery layer:** Compresses build time from months to weeks; small teams compete with large SIs.
- **Hiring:** Process mapping, agent capability matching, supervised handoff management — no ML PhD needed.
- **Pricing:** Discovery fee ($15–25k) funds prototype; retainer tied to outcome metrics, not hours.
