## What Happened

- **AIS Live (Online, July 11–12):** Nate's two-day virtual event — "Real AI Projects, Real Revenue" — structured as Build day (July 11) and Earn day (July 12). GA pricing from $147 through June 29. Sponsored by HyperAgent by Airtable. Recordings included; full refund guaranteed before day-two close.
- **Workless (Tivat, Montenegro, July 29–31):** An invite-only, in-person summit on the Adriatic for founders using AI to remove work from delivery, sales, support, and operations. Nate is a featured speaker alongside Liam Ottley, Dave Ebbelaar, Jack Roberts, and 20+ practitioners. Three days, one working room, one day at sea — Porto Montenegro.
- **Framing shift:** Nate's description for AIS Live is "for anyone tired of the hype around AI." Both events are explicitly practitioner-only in tone — revenue data and live builds, not framework decks.

## Nate's Take

The tutorial phase is winding down and the revenue phase is underway. Nate's choice to host AIS Live as a "Build then Earn" two-day structure — not a single keynote day — signals his view: understanding the tools is table stakes; knowing what businesses actually pay for and closing the invoice is the real gap. Workless doubles down on this with an invite-only filter: the room is founders shipping AI inside real businesses, not people learning what AI is.

## What It Means For Builders

- **Revenue data beats tutorials:** AIS Live's Day 1 is explicit — "the exact projects, with revenue data from practitioners selling them right now."
- **The voice AI interface signal:** Day 1 includes a "Voice as the next interface" slot — where natural-language AI is heading and what clients are about to expect.
- **Peer cohort, not content consumption:** The gap between knowing the tools and monetizing them is real — rooms with people who've crossed that line are the fastest way across it.
- **Online access democratizes the room:** AIS Live at $147 GA is accessible to the 350K free AIS members. No need to fly to Montenegro for the July 11–12 practitioner data.

## Steal This For Your Org

1. **Send one team member to AIS Live Day 1 (July 11).** The "Tool War" slot covers n8n vs Claude Code vs Make vs GHL with practitioner revenue data. $147 is a cheap research budget for a stack decision.
2. **Map the voice AI use case in your org before July 12.** Get a hypothesis on paper (which customer touchpoint, which workflow) to evaluate against what practitioners say on the day.
3. **Audit your team's "what have we built?" answer.** Each team member writes one concrete AI automation they shipped — not a prototype, a production system with a business outcome. This is the entry credential for the peer rooms Nate is building.
4. **Track the Workless speaker list for post-event content.** Create a simple watch-list of the 20+ speakers so practitioner signal doesn't get buried in the algorithm through August.

## Mistake to Avoid

Treating both events as networking or inspiration content and skipping the revenue-data sessions. Day 2 of AIS Live — discovery call playbook, pricing, retainer conversion — is the ROI, not the keynotes. If you can only attend one block, attend the sessions on what businesses are paying for right now and what the outreach looks like this quarter.
